  L(\beta_0,\beta_1,\sigma^2; {\bf y}) = \prod_{i=1}^n p(y_i|\beta_0,\beta_1,\sigma^2)
\]
where $p(y_i|\beta_0,\beta_1,\sigma^2) = \mathrm{Norm}(y_i|\mu_i,\sigma^2)$ and $\mu_i = \beta_0 + \beta_1x_i$
